Our Mission

Diplomacy in Action

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students represent different countries and debate global issues. At JFK MUN, we strive to foster a community of critical thinkers and eloquent speakers.


Whether you are interested in international relations, public speaking, or simply want to meet new people, JFK MUN offers a platform for everyone to grow.
