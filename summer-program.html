2026 Summer Program

Our beginner friendly summer program is designed to give middle schoolers the opportunity to learn more about Model United Nations in a relaxed environment with peers. We focus on research techniques, resolution drafting, and developing public speaking skills.


Schedule & Location

  • Dates: Tuesdays, June 16 – July 21
  • Time: 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Location: Millard Elementary School (5200 Valpey Park Ave)

Curriculum Overview

Date Focus Details
June 16 Introduction to MUN
  • Introductions & Icebreakers
  • MUN Basics & Committee Types
  • Essential Vocabulary
June 23 Position Papers & Speeches
  • Position Paper Writing Workshop
  • Opening Speech Practice
June 30 Resolutions & Teamwork
  • Resolution Drafting Activities
  • Working in Double Delegations
July 7 Moderated Caucuses
  • Rules of Procedure & Flow of Debate
  • Voice Projection & Speaking Tips
July 14 Unmods & Engagement
  • Unmoderated Caucus Strategies
  • Audience Engagement & Lobbying
July 21 Mock Conference

A full simulated United Nations conference followed by an awards ceremony.
